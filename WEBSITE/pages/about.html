Our
Journey
Established in 2021, Farbucks was created by two young entrepreneurs who
are still in college, introduce Matthew Anderson and Michael Vic.
Starting from making brewed coffee for his friends on campus, the
Farbucks coffeeshop was finally able to open several branches in several
countries, especially in Iceland, Indonesia and Russia.
First
Outlet
Before opening our first coffeeshop, we need a lot of things,
especially a milling machine and also tools to support the
production of premium coffee that we made. Therefore,
we are supported by the Iceland Government and are given
early modal and feasibility of business in the Reykjavik area.
